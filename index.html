§ 04

A Note on Method 方法小记 方法小记 A Note on Method

Version 1.0 · 2026 第一版 · 二〇二六
01 / NORMALISATION

Indexed to 100 归一为百 归一为百 Indexed to 100

Each dimension is aggregated from 4–7 sub-indicators, min–max normalised to a 0–100 scale. The leading entity on each axis does not necessarily score 100 — the ceiling reflects a theoretical maximum, not the current frontier. 每一维度由 4–7 个子指标加权汇总，按最大最小法归一为 0–100。各轴最高者未必得满分 — 顶值代表理论上限，而非当前领先水平。

02 / UNITS OF ANALYSIS

Asymmetric by Design 不对称乃本意 不对称乃本意 Asymmetric by Design

The eight entities are not commensurable in kind. A federal state (US), a supranational union (EU), a civilisation-state (CN), a bloc (ASEAN) and a continent (Africa) all appear side by side. Readers should treat each card as a portrait, not a ranking. 八大板块在类别上并不同构。联邦国家（美）、超国家联合（欧）、文明国家（中）、国家集团（东盟）与整片大陆（非洲）并列呈现。读者宜将每一版视作肖像，而非名次。

03 / SOURCES

Where the Numbers Come From 数据来源 数据来源 Where the Numbers Come From

Headline figures draw on IMF WEO, World Bank, UN DESA, OECD, WIPO, Freedom House, V-Dem, and national statistical offices. Figures are approximate and rounded; this atlas is for orientation, not policy. 主要数据引自国际货币基金组织、世界银行、联合国经社部、经合组织、世界知识产权组织、自由之家、V-Dem 及各国统计机构。数据皆为近似且经四舍五入，本图志仅供导览，不作政策依据。
